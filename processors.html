Questions

Producer FAQs

What does it cost to join?

Producers join free at launch. We earn a margin on orders that flow through the platform, so we only do well when you do — there's no upfront fee to list your products.

How does verification work?

We review your products and facility to confirm quality and origin. Verification is what lets retailers and shoppers trust what they're buying — and what lets you stand apart from unverified sellers.

Who handles delivery?

Our rider network handles delivery. You prepare orders for pickup — no need to build your own logistics. Delivery is tracked from pickup to the buyer's door.

What products can I list?

Mo'eats is a wellness platform — we carry health-positioned foods, from shelf-stable products like teas, superfoods, and cold-pressed oils to fresh options like smoothies and fruit bowls. If it's good for people, there's likely a place for it.
