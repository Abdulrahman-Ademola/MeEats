Questions

Rider FAQs

What do I need to start?

A means of transport (bike or motorbike), a smartphone, and valid ID. Join the waitlist and we'll walk you through verification and onboarding.

How and when do I get paid?

Earnings are transparent and tracked in the app in real time, with reliable payouts. You always see exactly what you've earned per delivery — no hidden deductions.

Can I choose my own hours?

Yes. Go online when you want to work and offline when you don't. The network is built around flexible, steady opportunity that fits your schedule.

Which cities are you in?

We're live in Lagos and Abuja and growing. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know as we expand into more areas near you.
