Questions

Retailer FAQs

What kinds of stores can join?

Pharmacies, gyms, organic and health shops, supermarkets, and wellness stores — any retailer that wants a reliable source of verified wellness products for their shelves.

Is there a minimum order?

Minimums vary by producer, but you build one order across many producers at once — so it's easy to hit thresholds while keeping your shelf varied.

How fast is restocking?

Delivery windows are shown clearly at checkout and tracked to your door. Reliable, on-time restocking is the core promise we hold ourselves to.

Can I see what sells before stocking?

Yes. Your dashboard surfaces what's trending in your area so you can stock with data, not guesswork — one of the biggest advantages of ordering through Mo'eats.
